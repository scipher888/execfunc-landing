The 5 core pieces

Fast to scan. Easy to understand when opened.

Each card uses the same simple pattern: what to say, what it does, why it helps, and what happens.

1

Sharpen the question

Prompt

Turns a rough question into a clear one before the real answer begins.

Just say

max prompt this

What it does

Adds a few short follow-up questions so the final answer can be more exact.

Why it helps

Clear questions lead to clearer answers. It cuts down on guesswork.

What happens

You answer a few quick prompts, then Synthia comes back with a sharper response.

2

Anchor in external reality

Ground

Checks outside facts, sources, or examples before answering.

Just say

ground this

What it does

Looks outward first so the answer starts from real information, not just a best guess.

Why it helps

It makes the answer feel more trustworthy, concrete, and tied to what is actually out there.

What happens

Synthia checks relevant outside material, then answers from a more grounded starting point.

3

Integrate multiple model perspectives

Council

More than one model looks at the question, then Synthia combines the strongest ideas.

Just say

council this

What it does

Gets separate takes from multiple models instead of relying on one first answer.

Why it helps

Different models notice different strengths, gaps, and possibilities.

What happens

Synthia compares the different outputs and gives you one stronger, integrated result.

4

Address complexity

Compute

Gives harder problems more time, more reasoning, and more care.

Just say

compute this

What it does

Uses a deeper reasoning path for messy, important, or high-stakes questions.

Why it helps

Complex problems usually need slower, more careful thinking than a quick first pass.

What happens

Synthia spends more effort on the problem and returns a more worked-through answer.

5

Choose the user's lens

Mode

Keeps the same question, but changes the style and lens of the answer.

Just say

in builder mode

What it does

Sets the lens for the full response without changing the question itself.

Why it helps

The best answer depends on the kind of help you want.

What happens

Synthia carries that lens through the answer from start to finish.

Common modes

Builder Practical and step by step.
Wisdom Reflective and values-aware.
Tension Holds both sides honestly.
Persuasion Focused on buy-in and influence.

Global Commands

Simple shortcuts for people who do not want to choose the setup.

The 5 pieces above are the core components. These commands simply choose the setup for you.

Global Command

Maximize

Run all 5 core pieces at maximum settings.

Just say

maximize

Best for

Questions that matter enough to justify the fullest possible pass.

What happens

Synthia turns on the full system and pushes every piece to its strongest setting.

Global Command

Optimize

Make a smart selection based on context.

Just say

optimize

Best for

When you want a strong answer without deciding which pieces to turn on.

What happens

Synthia looks at the question, picks the smartest mix, and uses the amount of each piece that fits the job.

Core pieces do the work. Global Commands choose the setup.
