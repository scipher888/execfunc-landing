The 4 pieces

Fast to scan closed. Easy to understand when opened.

1 · Ask clearer first

Max Prompt

Synthia asks follow-up questions before answering, so the real answer is much sharper.

Just say
max prompt this
What it is

A quick back-and-forth that turns a fuzzy question into a clear one before the real answer begins.

Why it matters

Vague questions usually get vague answers. This helps Synthia stop guessing and start understanding.

What happens

You ask. Synthia asks 5–9 short questions. You answer them. Then she gives you a much better response.

2 · Get multiple opinions

Council

Several separate AIs answer first. Synthia then combines the strongest ideas.

Just say
council this
What it is

Your question is answered by 3–4 different AI systems, each working on its own.

Why it matters

One answer can sound confident and still miss something. Multiple opinions reveal blind spots and better ideas.

What happens

Each AI answers separately. They do not copy each other. Synthia reads them all and gives you one stronger result.

Your questionSent to each AI
Separate answersDifferent takes, no copying
SynthiaCombines the best parts
Why people like it
You get a second, third, and fourth opinion instead of trusting one answer.
The most useful clue is often where the answers disagree.
3 · Go deepest when needed

Compute This

For hard questions, Synthia sends the job to the strongest AI available.

Just say
compute this
What it is

The “bring out the heavy equipment” option for questions that need more depth than usual.

Why it matters

Some questions are big, messy, or important enough that they deserve extra thinking time.

Human in the loop

Synthia prepares the prompt, then Dad or another manager pastes it into ChatGPT Pro and pastes the result back. That is on purpose for quality control and cost control.

4 · Change the angle

Modes

Same question. Different style of thinking.

Just say
in wisdom mode
What it is

A mode is like choosing the lens for the answer. Same topic, different style of thinking.

Why it matters

The best answer depends on what you need: practical steps, deeper meaning, balanced tension, or better persuasion.

What happens

You add the mode you want. Synthia approaches the whole request through that lens from start to finish.

🔧 Builder

The default. Practical, grounded, step by step. “What actually works?”

🌿 Wisdom

Meaning, values, life choices. “What matters here?”

⚖️ Tension

Holds both sides without forcing fake agreement. “What if both sides are right?”

🧲 Persuasion

Focused on influence and buy-in. “How do I convince someone?”

Same question, different mode

Example question: “How should I talk to my teenager about screen time?”

Builder mode: A practical conversation plan, likely sticking points, and what usually works at home.

Combining them

These pieces stack. You can use one, two, three, or all four together. The idea is simple: build the kind of help you want for this one question.
