Features

Everything you wish reminders could do

A complete executive function support system — not just another to-do list.

Personified AI Companion

Not a faceless app. A named AI with personality, memory, and understanding of your patterns. Research shows personified reminders improve task completion by 14+ points.

Escalating Interventions
